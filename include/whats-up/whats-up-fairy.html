Fairy trading port - Acting a show on the river at FUN ZONE


Gorgeous, eye-catching, lively, these are the feelings that visitors will utter when immersed in the show "Fair of the Fair" performed by "real masters" of Viet Tu at 20:00 every Friday night. , starting from June 28, 2019 to September 13, 2019 at FUN ZONE.


Once a trading port connecting Vietnam to the world, FUN ZONE recreates the ancient Hoi An port - one of the busiest ports in the East with the image of gathering 12 ships from other traders. each other, carrying the quintessence of the five continents. Soft silks, traditional ceramics or fruits and rice of the tropics became popular goods at that time, creating the cultural color of this place.


Not only is the bustling economic intersection, this is also the place to create a beautiful predestined relationship between Ngoc Hoa District and Japanese merchant - Asaki Sotaroi, helping to mark Vietnam's integration economy with the world. through the centuries.

Live show of THUAN TIEN PORT with a spectacular sound effect connecting the East and West banks will bring viewers back to the bustling trading port of the past with the cultural colors of the four directions, along with the romantic love story has become a symbol of cultural diversity.


Come to FUN ZONE every Friday night starting from June 28, 2019 to November 13, 2019 to immerse yourself in the unique show, admire the real scene on the shimmering river. end of the fireworks fireworks display.
