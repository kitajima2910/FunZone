Activities to celebrate National Day September 2, 2019 in Fun Zone.

The festival will take place on August 31 and September 1, 2019 at the Star Theater, with 4 performances. Morning from 10h00 to 12h30, and afternoon from 14h30 to 17h00 every day. The bands will perform international and domestic songs of Metal, Rock, Funk and Ballad. At the same time, there will be songs composed by the bands themselves.
An indispensable activity on National Day in Fun Zone 2019, is a fireworks display at 21:00 on September 2, 2019. This is one of the 3 points of the city firework. In addition to performing low-level float, Fun Zone also combines a huge laser show water screen, smoke screen on the lake. Light effects will create an image of a red star with a yellow star. In addition, there are performances on the lake before the fire point such as Noctilucent dragon with light dragons performing on the lake; Roboled dance; Fire drum ... In particular, visitors can take a boat on the lake to see this unique performance on the National Day.
