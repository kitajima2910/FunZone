Singapore water screen will be available at Fun Zone on November 27, 2019.

Is the water screen system ever seen at Marina Bay Sand Park (Singapore). This is a modern 3D projection technology on a giant 3D water screen. The system that creates a water screen called the Water Screen consists of a sprinkler system with powerful pressure nozzles. At the same time was installed by a team of experts from the lion island. When operating, the system will take water from Fun Zone to spray up to 20 meters high, and radiate around in a fan shape with a diameter of 45m, creating a giant 3D cubic screen with water.
Demo of Fun Zone water screen is coming soon
From here, people will show special designed films with high resolution, using the projector system on this 3D water screen. At the same time, around the lake is the 4-way sound system of the world famous brand: K2 (Czech Republic). Including 12 speaker clusters, each cluster consists of 2 speakers with a capacity of 600w to 2,000w per speaker. Particularly integrated control system IOT 4.0 designed by engineers of Alta Media Company.
