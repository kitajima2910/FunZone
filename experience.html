Area 1 of the "Fun Zone"

Expedition game - Lighthouse - Horror castle - Dinosaur park.

Dinosaur park

You will have an adventure about Jurassic. The habitat of giant prehistoric creatures. There are 110 dinosaurs of all kinds. You will encounter dinosaurs in the famous movie Jurassic Park (Jurasick Park). The Raptor will wait for you to attack when you come. Or you will be startled when the wild forest moves as if a T-Rex is about to appear.

Ice Area Game

This adventure game is for those who want to try the cold that only the Eskimos can live in. It exhibits 18 works of shimmering ice. The works reproduce many architectural works, landscapes of Vietnam and the world.

Horror castle

With images of ruined castle, the adventure game will bring you to the feeling of fear. Inside, you will meet the sleeping Dracula Count. This is a bloodsucker, sleeps coffin, lives at night and is afraid of sunlight. You will not be surprised when you see the mummies alive. Or open coffins, ghosts pretending to follow you. Try to explore the ghost of the castle that Dracula owns.

Area 2 of the "Fun Zone"

This is a breeding ground for wild animals. Including the orangutans Sumatra; Bear horse; Hippo; Elephants; zebra horse… The Fun Zone is trying to become a qualified member of the Association of Southeast Asian Zoos (SEAZA).

Hippo Africa

The hippopotamus is a semi-aquatic species, residing in rivers, lakes and mangrove swamps of West Africa where males occupy a stretch of river and are led by a group of 5 to 30 females and females. young. During the day, they maintain their coolness by soaking in water or mud; and reproduction also takes place in the country.

Bear horse

Sun bears have a length of about 1.30 - 1.90 m. Males weigh about 110 - 150 kg and females lighter, about 65 - 90 kg. Bear's life span is about 25 years. They live in mountainous forests of Southeast Asia, and at an altitude of 3,000 m. They have good climbing ability that helps them get fruits and nuts from the tree.

Sumatra orangutans at the Fun Zone zoo

As a large primate species close to the Indonesian people, they live only in Sumatra. They are 1.4 meters in size and average 90 kg in weight in males. The females are larger with an average size of 90 cm and weigh 45 kg. Compared to the Borneo orangutan, this species is thinner and has a longer face; Their fur is light red. This species is at high risk of extinction.

Yellow-cheeked gibbon

A native species of Vietnam, Laos and Cambodia. The adult male has black all-body hair with yellow cheeks, the female and the young have yellow hair. A recent report by the Wildlife Conservation Society indicates that there are about 2,500 individuals of the black-cheeked crested gibbon in the Cambodian Seima Biodiversity Conservation Area, where it is estimated to have the largest population of the black-cheeked crested gibbon in the world.

Area 3 of ​​"Fun Zone"

There are many interesting games including tram games, bullfighting arena, ferris wheel, aerial tram, lake boat, 8D film, racing, shooting, ...

Bumper car

A relaxing game with entertaining and fun. The trams are designed for one to two players, with rubber cuffs around them. So when the collision, the cars are not disturbed, but players feel excited. Currently in The Fun Zone has 2 areas for this game:

1. In the children's area called Ocean electric car, with the decoration in the style of the ocean

2. Next to the Bang Dang area with the name of the new generation Trams, with a space decorated with magnificent flowers.

Ferris stand

A sightseeing game with most amusement parks in the world, and one of the largest standing ferris in Ho Chi Minh City. Players sit in iron cabins, which are carried up in a circular motion at a slow pace. You will be watching the whole city when your cabin is at the top. Games for all ages.

Aerial train

Also known as Monorail, a type of sightseeing vehicle. This is a train running on a rail, walking around Dam Sen Lake, Roman Square, to the Nam Tu Thuong Uyen flower garden. You will admire the game parks and the beautiful flowers where your train passes. This is a relaxing game for you who want to travel to the Fun Zone.
