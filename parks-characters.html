The dragon is the head animal in the four spirits, the dragon represents the hexagram Chan, bringing the Yang Qi, the excavation, the will, the fame, fortune and power. Therefore, on the clothes of kings and royals often embroidered with golden dragons to represent heavenly beings with supreme power.
